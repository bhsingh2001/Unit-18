A little about me
When Im not designing
The conceptual evolution of everyday theories portrays that the development of technology is a never ending process. These advancements have resulted in the ability to now be able to actualise this progress, something that really sparks my attention. The disassembly and reassembly of radios, mobile phones, modification of soft/hardware were engaging to me as a child. Since then, my interest has only broadened to a degree of wanting to now create, design, and develop my own ideas through product design.
I continue to indulge in my passion for tinkering with electronics and engaging in mechanical work on cars. These hands-on pursuits provide me with a different kind of creative outlet, where I get to apply my technical skills and problem-solving abilities to real-world projects. Whether it's repairing and enhancing electronic gadgets or fine-tuning the inner workings of an automobile, I find immense satisfaction in the tangible results of my efforts. Additionally, I'm deeply connected to my cultural roots as an Indian classical musician, specializing in playing the tabla. Music has always been a source of inspiration and relaxation for me, allowing me to connect with the rich heritage of Indian classical music and express my creativity in a harmonious way. These diverse interests reflect my multifaceted personality and underscore my unwavering commitment to both innovation and tradition in all aspects of my life.
